About

Welcome to ZooParc Zoological Park

At ZooParc, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional wildlife experience while fostering a deep connection between people and nature. Spanning 70 hectares, our park is home to over 2,000 animals from 200 different species, including our beloved giant pandas. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and engage visitors through our commitment to animal care, conservation, and education.

Save their lives..

Our diverse collection of animals includes majestic lions, elegant bald eagles, vibrant poisonous frogs, gentle Asian elephants, and playful orangutans. Each species is carefully cared for by our dedicated team of veterinarians and zookeepers, ensuring their well-being and providing enriching experiences that mimic their natural habitats.
